# Strategic Sourcing Skill Gap Analysis

A structured leadership framework used to evaluate, map, and elevate the capabilities of procurement teams. This tool aligns individual talent competencies with macro supply chain goals and advanced digital procurement trends.

## 📈 Key Impact Metrics
* **Team Efficiency:** Boosted cross-functional project delivery speeds by 25%.
* **Talent Alignment:** Successfully mapped and upskilled a team managing diverse direct/indirect categories.
* **Strategic Shift:** Transitioned team focus from tactical "transactional buying" to value-driven "strategic sourcing."

---

## 🧭 Core Competency Matrix

This framework evaluates procurement professionals across four critical pillars:

### 1. Commercial & Analytical Acumen
* **Should-Cost Modeling:** Ability to dissect supplier cost structures.
* **Data Analytics:** Interpreting spend analytics, market indexes, and forecasting data.

### 2. Strategic Category Management
* **Market Intelligence:** Tracking commodity cycles, geopolitical risks, and supply constraints.
* **Supplier Relationship Management (SRM):** Developing collaborative innovation and risk-mitigation programs with tier-1 vendors.

### 3. Legal & Risk Mitigation
* **Contract Architecture:** Crafting robust SLAs, index-linked pricing clauses, and force majeure terms.
* **Compliance:** Ensuring adherence to environmental, social, and corporate governance (ESG) standards.

### 4. Soft Skills & Leadership
* **High-Stakes Negotiation:** Fact-based, win-win negotiation methodologies.
* **Stakeholder Management:** Aligning internal business heads (Finance, Operations, Quality) with sourcing strategies.

---

## 🛠️ The 3-Step Assessment Workflow

1. **Self-Assessment & Manager Review:** Team members and leadership rate current proficiency levels (1–5) across the core matrix.
2. **Gap Identification:** Comparing current capabilities against the future-state procurement requirements of the organization.

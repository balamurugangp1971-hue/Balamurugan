📐 Key Procurement & Inventory Formulas

Economic Order Quantity (EOQ)

EOQ = √(2 × D × S / H)
D = Annual Demand | S = Ordering Cost per Order | H = Holding Cost per Unit/Year
Optimize order size to minimize total inventory cost.

Reorder Point (ROP)

ROP = (Average Daily Usage × Lead Time) + Safety Stock
Determines when to trigger next purchase order to avoid stockout.

Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

TCO = Product Cost + Delivery + Quality Cost + Holding Cost − Discounts
Compare true cost across suppliers beyond just unit price.

Inventory Turnover Ratio

Inventory Turnover = COGS / Average Inventory Value
Higher ratio = better management. Industry benchmark: 8–12x/year.
